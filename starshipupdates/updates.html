SN15 Flight Delayed

The temporary flight restriction (TFR) for Saturday, May 1, 2021 for starship SN15's 10km was removed from the FAA's website. The current NET date is currently Sunday, May 2, 2021 with a TFR already in place. Teams will continue to watch the weather for Sunday. Still awaiting: road closures, marine hazard zones, village evacuation notices, and official confirmation from SpaceX.
